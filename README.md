# InternPhish
*Fake internships hurt for real.*
InternPhish is a community-driven platform that helps students identify, report, and verify internship opportunities to prevent scams. Many fake internships exploit desperate students by asking for registration fees, training payments, or deposits. InternPhish allows students to report such scams and warn others before they fall into the trap.

## The Problem

Fake internships are becoming increasingly common across platforms like:
- LinkedIn
- WhatsApp groups
- Telegram channels
- Email offers
- Student communities

Typical scam patterns include:
1. Asking for registration fees
2. Asking for training fees before joining
3. Promising stipends after payment
4. Mass referral chains where students promote scams
These scams exploit students who are searching for opportunities.

## The Solution

InternPhish provides a crowdsourced verification platform where students can:
- Report suspicious internships
- Check reviews before applying
- Upload evidence of scams
- View scam indicators and trust scores

The goal is simple:
Make internship scams harder to run and easier to expose.

Core Features
- Report an Internship
- Students can submit suspicious internship opportunities with details like:
1. Company name
2. Internship title
3. Source platform
4. Link or contact
5. Fee requirement
6. Description of the experience
7. Scam Indicators

InternPhish highlights common red flags such as:
- Payment required to join
- No official company domain email
- Unclear job role
- Referral based promotion chains

Each internship receives a Scam Probability Score.

Community Verification

Students can:
1. Upvote or downvote authenticity
2. Share experiences
3. Mark internships as:
Legitimate/Suspicious/Confirmed Scam

- Internship Lookup
Students can search for internship opportunities before applying and check:

1. Scam reports

2. Trust score

3. Community feedback

4. Evidence Upload

- Users can upload proof such as:

1. Payment screenshots

2. Emails

3. Offer letters

4. Chat conversations

This helps build transparency and credibility.
