Privacy Policy

Effective — May 1, 2025

What we collect

When you submit a report, we collect:

  • The organization name you provide
  • The scam message text you paste
  • An approximate fee amount (if provided)
  • Any additional notes you write

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information unless you voluntarily include it in the "notes" field.

What we do not collect

  • No cookies for tracking (we use no advertising or analytics cookies)
  • No login — no account, no password, no profile
  • No IP address stored beyond standard server logs
  • No payment data (we never ask you to pay anything)

How reports are used

Reports are made publicly searchable to serve InternPhish's core mission: helping job seekers identify fake companies. The scam message text you submit is displayed on the public Report Detail page. Do not paste any personal data about yourself or third parties into the message field.

Third parties

InternPhish does not share data with third parties for advertising or marketing. The site is hosted on standard web infrastructure. Google Fonts are loaded externally — see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data removal

If you submitted a report and wish to have it removed, email lalityadodla@gmail.com with the organization name and approximate submission date. We will process removal requests within 7 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
